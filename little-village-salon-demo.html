Our Salon

Friendly, local and beautifully straightforward.

The Little Village Salon is a well-loved local salon offering hair and beauty in a relaxed village setting. It’s the kind of place clients come back to because it feels welcoming, personal and easy.

This version of the website takes that same feeling and presents it in a cleaner, more refined way — with better structure, stronger visuals and a layout that feels much more established.

It is intentionally less “promo page” and more “real salon brand”.

Why this layout works

More white space, fewer boxed sections, stronger typography and a simpler navigation make the site feel more premium and more trustworthy.
