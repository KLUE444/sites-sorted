FAQ

Common Questions

Do you charge a call-out fee?
No. We never charge a call-out fee. You only pay for the work carried out. The hourly rate starts when we begin the job.
Are you Gas Safe registered?
Yes — all gas work is carried out by a fully registered Gas Safe engineer. You can check our registration on the Gas Safe website at any time.
How quickly can you get to me in an emergency?
For emergencies we aim to be with you within the hour depending on location. Call us directly on 07700 000000 for the fastest response.
Do you provide a quote before starting work?
Always. You'll receive a clear, fixed price before any work begins. If the scope of the job changes during the work, we'll always discuss it with you first.
What areas do you cover?
We cover Wisbech, March, Chatteris, Whittlesey, Ely, Downham Market and surrounding Fenland villages. If you're not sure, just give us a call.
Is your work guaranteed?
Yes — all labour is guaranteed for 12 months. Parts come with their manufacturer's warranty. If anything isn't right, we'll come back and fix it, no question.
