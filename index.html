No. That is the whole point. You send the basics, and we handle the setup, build, hosting, and launch.

Usually your business name, contact details, service list, service area, logo if you have one, and a few photos.

That depends on whether you choose one of the optional monthly care plans. These start from £15 per month for hosting, SSL, and minor updates.

Yes. Once paid for, the website content and branding are yours. We can also help manage the hosting if you want ongoing support.

What if I want changes later?

You can request updates through a care plan or ask for one-off changes if you are not on a monthly package.

How quickly can I get online?

Most simple websites can be turned around in 5–7 working days once we have your information and any images or wording needed.
