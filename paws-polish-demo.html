🤔 Got questions?

Things people ask us

🐾 How often should my dog be groomed?
Doodles and curly coats typically need grooming every 6–8 weeks. Double-coated and short-haired breeds can often go 10–12 weeks. We're always happy to advise on what suits your specific dog.
💚 Do you groom nervous or anxious dogs?
Absolutely! We specialise in gentle handling for nervous dogs. We work at their pace, never force anything, and use positive reinforcement throughout. Just mention it when you book so we can allow a bit of extra time.
⏰ How long does a groom take?
A bath and brush out is usually 1–1.5 hours. A full groom is typically 2–3 hours depending on breed, size and coat condition. We'll give you an estimate when you book so you know when to expect them back!
🐶 Do you do puppies?
Yes! We offer a gentle puppy introduction from around 12 weeks (once their vaccines are complete). Getting them used to grooming early makes such a big difference for the rest of their life.
🌿 What is a de-shed treatment?
It's a specialist treatment for heavy shedders like Labs and Retrievers. Using dedicated shampoo, conditioner and high-velocity drying, we remove a huge amount of loose undercoat — dramatically reducing shedding at home.
📅 How do I book?
Super easy! Just call, WhatsApp, message us on Facebook or use the contact form below. Tell us your dog's name, breed and what you're looking for and we'll get you booked in. We reply to all messages within a few hours.
