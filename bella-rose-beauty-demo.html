Questions answered

FAQs

What do I need to prepare?
Very little! Just a comfortable space to sit or lie down. I bring all the equipment, products and lighting needed. For nail treatments a table or lap tray is helpful.
How do I book an appointment?
The easiest way is to message via WhatsApp or use the contact form below. I'll get back to you quickly to confirm a time and date that suits you.
Do you charge for travel?
Travel within the Fenland area (Wisbech, March, Chatteris, Whittlesey and surrounds) is included in all treatment prices. A small travel charge may apply for locations further afield.
Are you fully qualified and insured?
Yes — I hold NVQ Level 3 in Beauty Therapy and am fully insured for all treatments I offer. Your safety and comfort is my priority.
How long do appointments take?
It depends on the treatment. A gel manicure typically takes around 45–60 minutes, lash extensions 1.5–2.5 hours, and a facial around an hour. I'll give you an accurate time estimate when you book.
Can I book multiple treatments?
Absolutely — many clients combine treatments in a single visit. Let me know what you'd like when you get in touch and I'll set aside the right amount of time.
